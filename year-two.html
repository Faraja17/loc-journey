Year Two

Toward the end of my first year, I started to experience serious thinning. I was very concerned that I would lose several locs. So, I took action. I did some research and learned about how my hair texture causes my hair to grow away from my locs, therefore I must retwist every 2-4 weeks. I also learned ways to save the already thinned locs. For the most part, I was successful! I only lost one loc. I had considered reattaching it, and I researched how to do so, but ultimately I decided to just discard it and work on growing what was left at the root to be healthy and strong.
